About Me

I am a student of logic and reasoning. What I enjoy most and desire in my work is the trial and error of problem solving. Using logical thinking and hard work to bring about a resolution to a problem, a reward to my diligence, is one of my greatest fixations. As a graduate with a degree in Neuroscience, I am no stranger to hard work, supported by my want of understanding. My switch to the web development field has been rewarding, allowing me to begin to work in a field that supports my passion for both understanding as well as the pursuit of the gratification that follows problem resolution. I am both an efficient solo worker as well as an active team player, allowing me to be flexible between independent work and a coordinated team effort. I have experience with JavaScript, Python, React/Redux, Flask, Express, HTML/CSS among other skills above. I hope that I can become even more proficient in the languages and frameworks I am already familiar with, as well as become well versed in new territories in web development in the future. Programming has quickly become my outlet for problem solving expression and I am excited for the opportunities that it holds.

Outside of work, my main hobbies include gaming, reading, and weightlifting. I was part of my college League of Legends team, and continue to grow as a member of the gaming community to this day. If you don't find me at my computer, I'm probably at the gym!
